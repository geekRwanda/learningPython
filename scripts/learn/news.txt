															<p>Miss Africa iteguwe ku nshuro ya kabiri, yafunguwe mu mwaka wa 2016 ihatanirwa n’abakobwa bo mu bihugu 18, ubu byariyongereye bigeze kuri 27 harimo n’u Rwanda.</p>
<p>Ibihugu byatumiwe uyu mwaka ni 36 gusa muri Calabar hagezeyo abakobwa 27 bo muri Algeria, Angola, Botswana, Burkina Faso, Burundi, Cameroon, Congo, Ethiopia, Gabon, Gambia, Ghana, Kenya, Morocco, Mozambique, Namibia, Nigeria, Rwanda, Sierra Leone, Somalia, South Africa, South Sudan, Swaziland, Tanzania, Zambia, Zimbabwe, Malawi na Mali.</p>
<p>Fiona Mutoni Naringwa waserukiye u Rwanda asanzwe ari umunyamideli ukomeye ndetse afite ikamba ry’igisonga cya Gatatu cya Miss Rwanda 2015. Mu mwaka ushize u Rwanda rwahagarariwe na Rachel Uwineza[umunyamideli wavukiye muri Kenya], icyo gihe ikamba ryahawe umukobwa wo muri Angola.</p>
<p>Ubu abakobwa bose batangiye gushakisha amajwi mu matora ari gukorerwa kuri telefone no kuri internet kuri WeChat no ku rubuga rwa Miss Africa. Guhesha Fiona Mutoni amahirwe yo kuboneka mu icumi ba mbere ni ukumutora ukoresheje ubutumwa bugufi wandika Migration Rwanda ukohereza kuri 35818[ku bari muri Nigeria] naho ku bari muri Afurika y’Epfo bohereza kuri 48108 naho abo muri Kenya bohereza kuri 22384. Abo mu bindi bihugu bamutora baciye kuri application ya WeChat[ushakishije Miss Africa].</p>
<p>Irushanwa rya Miss Africa rizasozwa ku itariki ya 27 Ukuboza 2017 ubwo hazaba hatorwa Nyampinga uzasimbura Umunya-Angola witwa Loretta Mendez.</p>
